Her favorite

She was so excited to see me. Gone for a year, playing soldier in the sand. The smoldering heat and blinding light of the desert paled in comparison to the smile she gave me. "I knew you would make it." She was the only person that could make me smile like that.

Forever in my heart. Living on in our memories.
